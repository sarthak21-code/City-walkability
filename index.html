Creating Cities That Walk Better

Improving daily life through clean streets, safer paths, and responsible consumption — aligned with SDG 12 & 12.5.

Why This Project?

Our cities are growing—but not always in the right direction. Footpaths break, crossings vanish, and walking becomes unsafe or uncomfortable. This creates more vehicle use, more pollution, more plastic waste, and more resource consumption.

Through this project, we explore how walkability directly supports SDG 12 by promoting responsible city living, reducing unnecessary waste generation, and creating healthier communities.

What We Aim To Show

  • Why walkability matters in modern cities
  • How poor walkability increases waste & pollution
  • Simple improvements that make walking easier
  • How walkability connects to SDG 12.5: reducing waste generation

A city becomes smart only when it becomes walkable first.
